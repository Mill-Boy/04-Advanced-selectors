After a lifetime of hunting in the jungle, I decided I was done taking lives of innocent creatures and therefore decided to get into the "Computer Programming BootCamp For Geniuses" at @Le Wagon.

Growing up in the brown mountains of South East Zimbabwe, My life dramatically changed after a face to face encounter with the great Lion that likes to go under the name of "Simba". Basically to cut long story short, that hairy freak wanted to eat me only because I had killed Pumba to make a tasty incredible Bacon/Mayo sandwich to my girlfriend, "Mala". I won't lie, I STILL can"t understand his reaction but actually it did change the course of things for me since I'm here coding with great people and a great system invented a few years ago that they like to call "WIFI" so I'll always be grateful for that event. May his soul rest in the animal Heaven, if there is one of course.
